# CERT-In Advisory Sample Ransomware Campaign

CERT-In style advisories describe observed campaigns, affected sectors, indicators of compromise, recommended mitigations, and reporting guidance. Ransomware advisories commonly emphasize phishing prevention, exposed remote access hardening, offline backups, endpoint detection, and incident reporting.

Recommended mitigations include patching internet-facing systems, enforcing MFA, disabling unused services, maintaining tested backups, monitoring suspicious PowerShell activity, and blocking known malicious infrastructure.

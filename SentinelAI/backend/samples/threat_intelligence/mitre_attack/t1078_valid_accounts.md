# MITRE ATT&CK T1078 Valid Accounts

Adversaries may obtain and abuse valid accounts to maintain access, evade detection, or move laterally. This technique is frequently associated with credential theft, phishing, password spraying, token theft, and abuse of dormant accounts.

Detection guidance includes monitoring impossible travel, unusual login times, new device fingerprints, abnormal source IP ranges, privilege changes, and access to systems outside a user's normal baseline.

Response guidance includes disabling compromised accounts, revoking active sessions, forcing credential rotation, reviewing MFA status, and auditing recent account activity.
